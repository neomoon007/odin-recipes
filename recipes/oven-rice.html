Oven Rice

An oven rice it's like a lasagna but made with rice, it's a delicious dish that we like to make for our family on sundays.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of cooked rice
  • 1 can of vegetables of your like
  • 1 sheredded chicken breast
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 1 chopped onion in slices
  • 1/2 cup of grated mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix the content of the can of vegetables with the cooked rice, without heating it up.
  2. Saute the onion in oil, put the chopped tomatoes, the chicken breast and the 2 tablespoons of tomato sauce.
  3. Put salt as you want.
  4. In a refractory put a layer of rice, sauce and repeat until finish with a layer of rice.
  5. Above the rice, put the grated cheese.
  6. Heat it up on medium heat on the oven for 15 minutes.
