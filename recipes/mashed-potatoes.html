Mashed Potatoes

This is an easy mashed potatoes recipe, very simples but still delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 cubes chicken bouillon
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons milk

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into 1-inch cubes. Add to a multi-functional pressure cooker with water and bouillon cubes. Select Saute function; set timer for 4 minutes on High.
  2. Mix potatoes and water and break up thee bouillon cubes. Close and lock the lid. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 10 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
  3. Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, about 10 minutes. Release remaining pressure careully using the quick-release method. Remove the pot and carefully drain off most of the liquid.
  4. Add sour cream, butter and milk. Gently mash with a potato masher to reach your desired consistency.
